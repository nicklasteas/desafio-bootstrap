About Sukeban Games

We are an independent game developer originally from Venezuela, now spread across the world looking for better horizons. Our work focuses on the intricacies of being alive.

The name sort of means “delinquent boss girl”, and comes from 70’s japanese delinquent lingo and exploitation films from the era. It represents an attitude that aims to always be challenging, and a testament to an undying love for the pop culture that shaped our creative lives.

Current team: @kiririn51 (direction), @juneji_ (music, sfx), @merenge_doll (art), NewProgrammer (programming, no social media accounts).
